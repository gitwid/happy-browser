# Happy Labs Research Testbeds

Status: active research harnesses  
Purpose: falsify load-bearing concepts before implementation hardens them

These testbeds exist to keep the Happy Labs continuity research constructable.
They are not product requirements and they are not implementation designs.

Each testbed should answer one question:

> Does this concept survive contact with a concrete example without losing its
> category boundaries?

## Immediate Testbeds

1. [Legitimacy Transition Algebra](legitimacy-transition-algebra.md)
2. [Subjective Evidence Taxonomy](subjective-evidence-taxonomy.md)
3. [Reverse Traversal Closure](reverse-traversal-closure.md)

## Admission Rule

Add a testbed only when a concept is either:

- Load-bearing but under-substantiated.
- At risk of becoming architectural kudzu.
- Easy to confuse with a neighboring concept.

## Non-Goals

- Do not create agent roles here.
- Do not invent storage schemas here.
- Do not turn unresolved concepts into product backlog items.
- Do not add testbeds for every interesting analogy.

## Evaluation Vocabulary

- Pass: the concept preserves its boundaries on the fixture.
- Partial: the concept works but reveals a missing predicate, state, or evidence
  class.
- Fail: the concept collapses into a neighboring concept or cannot explain the
  fixture.
- Deferred: the fixture lacks enough evidence to evaluate the concept.
- Kudzu risk: the concept starts requiring machinery that is not justified by
  the fixture.
