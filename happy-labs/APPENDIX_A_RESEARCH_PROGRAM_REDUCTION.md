# Appendix A - Research Program Reduction

Discipline: Legitimacy Instrumentation

## Status Change

The Palindromic Crown Algorithm is not currently pursued as a unified explanatory theory.

It is retained as a speculative theoretical substrate whose purpose is to generate independently testable instruments.

The framework exists to produce instruments.

The instruments do not exist to defend the framework.

## Active Engineering Projects

### 1. Round-Trip Drift Metric

Status: buildable now.

Purpose:

Measure semantic or normative degradation across forward and reverse traversal.

Initial success criterion:

A practitioner can apply the metric to a corpus without needing to accept PCA.

External comparators:

- rate-distortion theory
- information preservation
- entailment preservation
- task-completion preservation

This is the first empirical project.

### 2. Receipt Discriminator

Status: hardest, highest leverage.

Purpose:

Determine whether an authorization-compatible artifact actually traversed a legitimate receipt process.

Former name:

Forgery-gap discriminator.

Reason for renaming:

Forgery is only one failure mode.

Relevant failure modes include:

- adversarial forgery
- imitation
- shortcuting
- proxy optimization
- accidental convergence

The invariant is receipt, not forgery.

Initial research target:

Proxy optimization, because it is measurable in deployed AI-like systems and has empirical comparators.

Layer 3 of the business strategy - instrumentation as infrastructure - is gated on this instrument.

## Boundary Conditions

### Legitimacy Predicate

Status: boundary condition, not an instrument.

Purpose:

Define whether a transition has been received, reviewed, delegated, refused, or accepted under human authority.

Constraint:

No amount of recursive closure substitutes for human receipt.

Human receipt, refusal, and delegation must remain first-class predicates.

PCA is not viable as an authorization theory unless this condition is explicit.

## Deferred Theoretical Scaffolding

### Synchronization Detector

Status: deferred.

Reason:

It depends on an unresolved stopping condition.

Until convergence can be defined, synchronization detection risks becoming a relabeled palindrome.

Deferred question:

When has bidirectional simulation converged enough to count as synchronization?

## Anti-Fragility Criterion

An instrument graduates only when a practitioner in an adjacent field can use, critique, or improve it without reading the PCA paper.

Adjacent fields include:

- information theory
- authentication
- distributed systems
- type theory
- institutional analysis
- security UX
- informed authorization

Survival without the framework label is insufficient.

Survival without the framework's private vocabulary is required.

## Legitimacy Logic

Status: conjectural.

Business logic may be a special case of legitimacy logic.

Business logic governs permitted transitions.

Legitimacy logic governs warranted transitions.

However, this claim must not become a new grand theory by default.

Each proposed projection must earn its place by naming at least one observable.

Candidate projections:

- business process
- scientific reasoning
- personal continuity
- governance
- authentication
- AI evaluation

If no observable can be named, the projection remains a hypothesis.

## Comparative Pressure

The research program must increasingly face external standards.

Recommended comparisons:

- Round-trip drift: information theory
- Receipt discrimination: authentication and attestation
- Synchronization detection: distributed systems
- Legitimacy predicates: type theory and refinement types
- Human receipt: informed authorization, consent, and security UX
- Institutional legitimacy: institutional analysis

The objective is not internal elegance.

The objective is comparative explanatory and instrumental power.

## Strategic Frame

The business progression is:

1. Continuity systems record.
2. Legitimacy systems justify.
3. Instrumentation systems evaluate.

The third layer has platform potential only if receipt discrimination proves constructable.

Measurement is sellable.

Theory is not.

## Current Instruction

Do not ask:

"Is PCA true?"

Ask:

"What instrument can be built, measured, falsified, and used outside PCA?"

The research program succeeds when fewer concepts require belief and more concepts require measurement.

Core state change:

Legitimacy instrumentation stays.

PCA is demoted.

Drift metric and receipt discriminator become the active projects.

Legitimacy predicate becomes a boundary condition.

Synchronization detector is deferred until stopping-condition work exists.
